Lebanese Fattoush Salad

Description:

It is the dish that you will find it on every table in the Lebanese culture.

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Parsley
  • Tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Cucumbers
  • Green onions
  • Radishes
  • Pita bread
  • Salad dressing

Steps:

  1. Chop the ingredients into small pieces.
  2. Fry the pita bread pieces.
  3. Start building the fattoush salad by adding all the chopped ingredients to a large bowl.
  4. Pour the dressing on top.
  5. Toss everything to combine well. But keep the pita bread on the side so that it can be added when served to stay crisp.
