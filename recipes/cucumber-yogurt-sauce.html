Cucumber Yogurt Sauce

Description:

This fresh and cooling dish is easily made with simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Yogurt
  • Water
  • Cucumber
  • Mint
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Whisk together the yogurt and water until it's smooth and consistent.
  2. Stir in the rest of the ingredients to combine.
  3. season the dish with olive oil.
